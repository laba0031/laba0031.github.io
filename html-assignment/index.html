About Me

My name is Mikayla. I'm from Keswick, a small town north of Toronto. I'm a surrealist conceptual photographer. Most of my body of work is self portraits but I'm starting to branch out to doing portraits of other people as well. I've started making a bucket list and right at the top is to do a photo shoot with Lady Gaga.
