About Me

My name is Mikayla. My initals are MEL. I'm from Keswick, a small town north of Toronto. I'm a surrealist conceptual photographer. Most of my body of work is self portraits but I'm starting to branch out to doing portraits of other people as well. I've started making a bucket list and right at the top is to do a photo shoot with Lady Gaga.

Today is currently September 27th 2021. I am studying Interactive Media Design, at Algonquin College in Ottawa. Using the skills provided from this program, I hope to be able to work for myself one day. Not sure what I want to do yet, hopefully stay a photographer but would love to be able to add more skills to my resume.
